Fire

With fire we consume and exhume, christened with heat that we lost in the womb. With fire we dance, we prance and we sing. Fire is a wonderful thing.

Love

Hearts in battle, in our chests they rattle, stuck in a cage, related to rage. With love we run, with love we hide. With love we hate, with love we relate. A frozen pond, or a concrete lake. We sing a song, of loving mates. Feel the tune, and don't leave soon. Unpack your bags, stay here with me. We might see what we can be.
